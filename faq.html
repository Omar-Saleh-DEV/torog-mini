Frequently Asked Questions

Toronet Governance Tokens (TOROGs) are used for participating in the governance of the Toronet platform, including deploying smart contracts and admitting community nodes.

You can create a Toronet wallet by following the step-by-step guide on our website.

Request the number of TOROGs you would like and get whitelisted through our request form.

TOROGs are intended for use as a governance utility of the Toronet platform, deploying smart contracts, admitting community nodes, and enabling the community to shape the direction of the platform. They are not intended as a security, and there is no promise implied or expressed of TOROGs growing in value as a token.

You can get support by contacting our support team through the contact page on our website or by joining our community on Discord.
